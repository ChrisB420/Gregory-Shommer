Our Heart for Missions

The vision of Wings of the Cherubim extends beyond teaching to active evangelism and support for communities in need. Through the leading of the Holy Spirit, we are committed to practical outreach that shares the Gospel and demonstrates God's love.

The Mombasa T-Shirt Ministry, Kenya

One of our foundational outreach initiatives is the distribution of T-shirts in **Mombasa, Kenya**. These T-shirts are not merely garments; they are powerful tools of evangelism, bearing the name of **Jesus Christ** and foundational Bible verses like **John 3:16** in Swahili.

This ministry, managed by our dedicated contact Vanessa in Mombasa, directly impacts lives by providing clothing while simultaneously sowing seeds of the Gospel. It's a tangible way to spread the truth and love of Christ in a vibrant community.

"For God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life." - John 3:16 (Swahili: "Mungu alipenda ulimwengu hata akamtoa Mwanawe wa pekee, ili kila amwaminiye asipotee bali awe na uzima wa milele.")
